# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 095
**Title:** Lost in Translation

## The Death (One Sentence)
A language learning app enthusiast misreads a warning sign in Japanese, eats a deadly wild plant while foraging, and dies believing she's having an authentic cultural experience.

## Tagline
"She thought she was fluent. She was fatally wrong."

## Themes
1. The danger of overconfidence in partial language skills
2. Apps and technology as false safety nets
3. The gap between tourist Japanese and actual fluency

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 45%
- Horror: 30%

## Target Reader
Travelers who think 50 days of Duolingo makes them fluent, who order in broken local languages while locals wince, who trust translation apps over native speakers. Anyone who has ever confidently said something wrong in a foreign language.
